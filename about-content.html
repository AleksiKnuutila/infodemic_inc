What is Information Disorder Inc.?

Information Disorder Inc. is an educational game where you play a manager in a social media platform company. You will confront many dilemmas, such as how to deal with images created by generative AI and what kind of content will be permitted on the platform. See if you can run the company without a crisis due to the platform's reputation crashing or becoming unprofitable.

How does the game work?

The game can be played on mobile phones and computers in your web browser. The game looks like a chat interface in which you must choose responses to steer the company's direction. Keep an eye on the company's "profit" and "reputation" and keep these above zero to avoid a crisis.

Who should play this game?

Information Disorder Inc. was created with a broad audience in mind and is suitable for anyone over the age of 12. The game contains some references to verbal violence, such as slurs, but no swear words or rude language is used. Real-world events inspired the game content, but many narrative details have been changed. The game was developed as a media literacy tool that creates interest in and teaches social media and concepts related to platform governance.

Who is behind Infodemic Inc?

A consortium of the University of Helsinki, the University of Oxford and the University of Florence developed the game. The game is a part of the project InfoLead – Information and media literacy programme for judges and policymakers, funded by the European Media and Information Fund. Big Blue Comms did the technical implementation and graphic design.

Data Protection

We track how many people have played the game but do this anonymously. We don't collect any information about people visiting the website or playing the game that would make it possible to identify them.

Open Source

The code for this game is available as an open source, and can be adapted for other content. You can view, fork, or contribute to the codebase at https://github.com/AleksiKnuutila/infodemic_inc.

El juego se puede jugar en teléfonos móviles y computadoras en tu navegador web. El juego tiene una interfaz de chat en la que debes elegir respuestas para dirigir el rumbo de la empresa. Mantén un ojo en la "ganancia" y la "reputación" de la empresa y mantén estos valores por encima de cero para evitar una crisis.
